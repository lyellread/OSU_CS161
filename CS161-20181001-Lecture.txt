Defenitions
Pre Increment/Decrement: var2=++var1 : var1 will be incremented then stored in var2
Post Increment/Decrement: var2=var1-- : var1 will be stored into var2 and then var1 will be decremented. NOTE: var2 WILL NOT BE DECREMENTED!
End_Defenitions

Concepts
Pre, Post Increments (++): Pre: ++<var>; Post: <var>++
Pre, Post Decrements (--): Pre: --<var>; Post: <var>--
Switch Statements: switch(expression){case1:, case2: default:...}
End_Concepts

Notes
Variables and Scope: Variables exist only in the scope of the block they are created in. A block is {..}.
End_Notes
